Dell Pro Docks

Pro Smart Dock SD25 (USB-C)

Full Experience

(When used with a Dell Laptop)

  • Basic Connections
  • Max Charging: 130W
  • Network Speed: 2.5 Gbps
  • Laptop Power Button Sync
  • Advanced IT Management

Limited Experience

(When used with other brands)

  • Basic Connections
  • Max Charging: 96W (Downgraded)
  • Network Speed: 2.5 Gbps
  • Laptop Power Button Sync
  • Advanced IT Management

Downgraded/Lost features include:

  • Fleet Management (DDMC): Full remote management requires Dell systems.
  • Partial Universal Support: Unlike other docks, Dell notes that MAC Pass-Through and WOL *may* function on some non-Dell systems, but it is not guaranteed.
  • Max Charging: Limited to 96W (down from 130W).
